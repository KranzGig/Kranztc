Privacy Policy

At documenthours.com, we care about your privacy and understand that it is important to you.

That's why we've created this simple privacy policy to explain how we handle your information. Please take a moment to read through it.

Information we collect
We only collect the bare minimum of information necessary (name, email address and phone number) for the site to function properly and to allow us to maintain it. We do not track you or use cookies.

How we use your information
We use hostinger.com to store the information entered. We use Twilio to send you text message reminders. The weekly hour information for you is sent to the accountant / admin so that you can be paid. Your information may be used as we develop and test new features and bug fixes. Your information is not sold or used for marketing in any way.

Data security
We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. All data stored in your browser is kept secure and is not accessible by anyone else.

Changes to this policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, so please check back periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Contact us
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the way we handle your information, please feel free to contact us at admin@kranztc.com.
